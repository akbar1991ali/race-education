About Us

We are bunch of people who started teaching with motive to make education available to those who are not very privileged economically but deserve equal chance to learn.

We offer a full range of accredited online courses from grades 9-12. Learn when and where you want, with Ontario teachers and guidance counsellors supporting you along the way.

Why choose Us?

Choose it for your online learning because it’s flexible, affordable and accredited. Whether you need to earn credits, boost a grade or obtain a high school diploma, ILC helps you succeed!
